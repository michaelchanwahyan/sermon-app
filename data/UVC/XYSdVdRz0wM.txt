and let our hearts truly return to Him.。
Let us pray with our hearts.。
God, we know that Your way。
and Your great command。
is not what we humans think.。
Your ultimate goal。
is to make our lives belong to You.。
God, we pray that You take away。
the things we long for in our lives。
like stability and prosperity。
and the benefits that the land can give us.。
God, let our lives truly belong to You.。
Let You be our true Lord.。
You continue to lead our lives。
and the path ahead of us.。
No matter what we are going through,。
let us know that the ultimate goal。
is not only to be free and carefree。
but to be faithful to You.。
Continue to train us,。
continue to help us,。
and nurture us。
so that we can become。
more fit for Your will.。
Let us pray.。
God bless you.。
